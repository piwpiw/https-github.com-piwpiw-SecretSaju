# Vision & Mission - Secret Saju

## Vision
Create the leading AI-assisted fortune platform in Korea by combining accurate traditional computation, transparent monetization, and measurable product outcomes.

## Mission
- Deliver trusted calculations with explainable results.
- Build repeatable growth through daily habits and social sharing.
- Maintain product quality standards that scale across paid and free tiers.

## Positioning
- 고정확도 + 즉시성 + 바이럴 공유성의 세 가지를 동시 만족하는 사주 플랫폼을 지향한다.
- 핵심 성공 기준은 수익성만이 아니라 재방문/신뢰 루프의 동시 개선이다.

## Success Signals
- Conversion clarity: each paid action has one clear next step.
- Trust: users can always see why a result is generated and how to unlock deeper content.
- Responsiveness: mobile-first by default.
