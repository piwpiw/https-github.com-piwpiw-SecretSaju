## Rules

- Keep terminology consistent with current product screens.
- Do not duplicate route contracts in content docs.
- Put strategic history in archive docs, not live operating docs.
- Coordinate with engineering when copy changes require new message mappings.

## Deliverables

- Archetype copy
- Prompt and content templates
- UX copy for auth, payment, and result flows
- QA-reviewed wording updates
