## 🎯 The Opportunity

Secret Saju is redefining the $2B+ Korean fortune-telling market with **enterprise-grade technology** and a **proven freemium model**.

### The Problem
- Traditional saju services: Offline, expensive ($50-200/session), inconsistent quality
- Existing apps: Low accuracy, poor UX, no monetization
- **Market gap**: No premium, tech-first saju platform

### Our Solution
- **Enterprise-grade calculation**: 60-Ganji accuracy with True Solar Time correction
- **Freemium model**: Free trial → social unlock → premium ($1-10)
- **Viral mechanics**: Social sharing + gamification built-in
